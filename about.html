About PC Mini Golf


  • PCMiniGolf has a mission to create a PEOPLE CONNECTING ADVENTURE that includes 6-96 year olds. We provide something to do while connecting with old friends, making new friends and celebrating life events. Our fun mini golf course is an activity to do with coworkers, party guests, family reunion times and team building. We deliver, set up the course, come back and remove it. Our business is family owned and operated. The business hopes to help everyone find more ways to socialize and connect. Reach out today with any questions and we will be happy to help create a great experience with mini golf.
  • PC Mini Golf is a family-owned and opperated business based in Park City Utah. We have up to 9 mini golf holes that we deliver to various events. With equipment for all ages, PC Mini Golf is a great option to increase the fun at any event.
