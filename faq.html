Frequently Asked Questions


  • Question: How much space is needed to host the minigolf course?

    Approximately 200sq feet for the 3-hole, 400sq feet for the 6-hole, and 600sq feet for the 9-hole.
  • Question: How many people can play mini-golf at once?

    We have 16 adult-sized clubs and 14 children-sized clubs, so if it is a mixed group 30 people could start with groups starting at each hole.
  • Question: How long does it take to play the 9 holes?

    Typically groups of four take about 40-50 minutes to play; children are somewhat faster.
  • Question: Can children and adults play together?

    Yes, this is an excellent activity for children 6 years old and up to play with adults of any age.
  • Question: Does it work best inside or outside?

    It is best played indoors. A community room, ballroom, big concrete lobby or patio, or a hard surface multiple room setup works the best for playing. Outside is possible on a deck, patio, or level driveway if needed.
  • Question: Can someone host the mini golf or guide my guests through it for me?

    We have people to act as "coaches", start-guides, or "course directors" for $20 an hour.
  • Question: If all nine holes do not fit in my space, can I rent less?

    Yes, we are happy to discuss your party and space and find a good solution that fits!
  • Question: Can the balls break something?

    Yes, these are 'real golf balls' and overly or fully swinging a golf club could cause a ball to break something or dent or hurt, so mini-golf gentleness is key to the enjoyment of the course. We have foam balls as an option for play. They are also a fun course experience.
  • Question: How long does it take to set up the course and take it down?

    Depending on how far from the vehicle the playing space is we can usually set up in 90-120 minutes and take down is about the same as set up.
